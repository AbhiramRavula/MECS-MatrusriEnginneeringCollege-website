OUR VISION

To be a premier academic institution striving continuosly for execellence in technical education, research and technological service to the nation.

OUR MISSION

  • To recognize and make quality Engineering education available to the society.
  • To create a communtiy of learning in which students acquire knowledge and apply them professionally with societal concern.
  • To prepare for life-long learning to meet intellectual and career challenges.
  • To promore professional ethics, leadership qualities and social responsibilites.
  • To maintain research environment with an opportunity to create, apply and disseminate knowledge to the needs of society anf the industry.
